#Security Policy

## Reporting Vulnerabilities
We take the security of our software seriously and appreciate your efforts to responsibly disclose vulnerabilities. 
If you discover a security issue or potential vulnerability in this project, please follow these steps:

1. **Do not publicly disclose** the vulnerability until it has been reviewed and resolved. 
2. **Report it privately** by emailing the maintainer directly. 
   - Email: gfs7337@nyu.edu 
   - Alternatively, you may contact through GitHub private message 
3. Include detailed information in your report:
   - Steps to reproduce the issue 
   - Affected files, configurations, or components
   - Potential impact 
   - Suggested remediation

Please allow up to **3 business days** for an update on the status and expected resolution timeline.

## Security Update Policy
We follow the principles of responsible disclosure and timely patching:

- **Critical vulnerabilities**: Patched and released within **48–72 hours** of verification. 
- **High/Medium vulnerabilities**: Fixed in the next scheduled update or within **1 week**. 
- **Low-severity issues**: Addressed in routine maintenance or minor updates. 
- **End-of-life versions**: No security patches or updates are provided.
